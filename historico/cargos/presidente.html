Details for PRESIDENTE Cargo

The PR-D Cargo is a prestigious position within the IFMSA Brazil organization. It was established to ensure the smooth communication between the local and international teams, representing Brazil on the global stage.

Over the years, the role has evolved, but the essence remains the same: to serve as the bridge between Brazil and the rest of the IFMSA community. The PR-D is responsible for attending international meetings, liaising with other countries, and ensuring that Brazil's voice is heard.

Many illustrious members have held the PR-D position in the past, and their contributions have significantly shaped the trajectory of IFMSA Brazil.
